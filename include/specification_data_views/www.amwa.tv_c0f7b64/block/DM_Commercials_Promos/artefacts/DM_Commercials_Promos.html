Element cd:Content_Details / cd:Audio_Loudness_Details / cd:Programme_Loudness_Value
Namespace http://www.amwa.tv/projects/rules/01/block/DM_ContentDetails/
Annotations
The "Programme Loudness Value" in LKFS (also knows as "LUFS") as measured in accordance with the indicated Audio Loudness Specification. It will not always be possible to reproduce the measurement without further information such as which audio channels were measured, what anchor was used, or if part of the content was excluded from the measurement. This is intended to give an indication of the degree to which the content deviates from the indicated Audio Loudness Specification.
